    The Showroom system

    Five hues off the mid-century floor — seafoam and fern upholstery, brass hardware, rust enamel, walnut everywhere — set over gallery bone by day and den walnut after hours. One fluid slab-serif hierarchy, solved with strict clamp() math on bare semantic tags, keeps every measure readable from a phone at the bench to a drafting monitor.

    01 · Color

    A furniture showroom, not a color wheel

    Each hue earns a job the contrast math can defend. Rust is the only color allowed to ask for a click in daylight; walnut is the only neutral; seafoam and fern stay quiet until dark, where seafoam becomes the interactive voice.

    The Habitat 26 nightstand

    A specimen article set entirely by element defaults — July 2026

    Describe a piece in plain language and Blueprint Buddy answers with a professional-grade plan: an interactive preview, a structural report grounded in real materials science, and a cut list with joinery allowances. The engine treats MOE as the stiffness authority, holds a ×4 safety factor, and prints every formula it used — tap a number like 26 in to see its work.

    Structure passes before style

    Table-like pieces are judged as frames, not a lone top: apron beams, the top strip between aprons, then the overhang cantilever. Sustained loads carry the ×2 creep factor from the Wood Handbook, chapter 4.†

    What the judge checks

    • Sag margin at safety factor 4 — MOR governs strength;
    • tipping with every drawer open, aligned with ASTM F2057;
    • seasonal movement: width × coefficient × ΔMC, attachment-aware.

    3. Taper the two inside faces only.

    The contract

    • Zoom beats viewport. Clamp bounds are rem; at 200% zoom the rem terms double while vw stands still — text always at least doubles (WCAG 1.4.4).
    • Measure holds 45–75ch for prose at every width; tables and ledes get their own caps.
    • Leading is additive — calc(1em + n·rem) — so it scales with zoom and self-tightens as display sizes grow.
    • No color stands alone. Verdicts are stamps with text; rust doubles as failure only alongside a label.
    • Reduced motion, higher contrast, forced colors each get a media block: transitions die, hairlines harden to ink, swatches opt out of remap only where color is the content.
