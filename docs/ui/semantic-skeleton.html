    First-fit-decreasing packing, 3 mm kerf, 15 mm end trim. Board feet stay a reference line.

    1. Mill the legs

      Rough, rest, then S4S to 1¾ in square. Mark tapers from the apron line down; taper the two inside faces only.

    2. Mortise and tenon the aprons

      Setout is snapped to a ¼ in chisel; blind tenon depth is capped by the leg. Drilling positions match the joinery-detail table exactly.

    3. Glue up the base

      Check both diagonals before the glue sets — the racking score assumes a square base.

    4. Fit the drawer

      Half-blind dovetails at the front, ¹⁄₁₆ in reveal per side. Wax the runners after finishing.

    5. Finish

      Grit ladder 120 → 180 → 220, then three coats of Danish oil. Lay oil-soaked rags flat to dry — piled rags can self-ignite.
