Blueprint Buddy is a tool for designing furniture. This policy explains what information the service handles, why, and the choices you have. We have tried to collect as little as the product needs to work.

The short version. You can use Blueprint Buddy without an account — your designs stay on your own device. If you sign in, we store your designs so they follow you across devices. We use Stripe for payments and Anthropic to power the AI. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not run third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

2. Information we handle

If you use the app without signing in

Your designs, preferences, and prices are saved in your browser (local storage) on your device. They are not transmitted to us and we cannot read them.

Anonymous AI usage (rate limiting)

To keep the free AI available to everyone and prevent abuse, we count AI requests from anonymous users. We do this against a one-way hash of your IP address — we do not store your raw IP address for this purpose. The counter resets on a rolling schedule and is not used to identify you.

If you create an account

You sign in through Google or GitHub (OAuth). We receive from that provider your basic profile information — typically your name, email address, a provider account identifier, and avatar URL. We do not receive your password. We use this to create and secure your account and to associate your saved work with you.

Your saved designs (with an account)

When you are signed in, the furniture designs and project data you create are stored on our behalf by our cloud data provider so they sync across your devices. This content is scoped to your account identifier and is not shared with other users.

Payments (Pro subscriptions)

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number — Stripe handles card data directly under its own security standards. We store your Stripe customer identifier and your subscription status (for example, active or canceled) so we can unlock Pro features for your account.

AI-generated designs and photos

When you use the AI chat or the photo-to-design feature, the text you send and any photo you upload are transmitted to our AI provider, Anthropic, to generate a design in response. Send only images you are comfortable sharing for this purpose. If the AI is not configured, the app falls back to a built-in offline parser and no data leaves the service for that request.

Essential cookies

If you sign in, we set a single session cookie that is cryptographically signed so it cannot be tampered with. It exists only to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies, and we do not embed third-party analytics or tracking scripts.

3. How we use information

  • To provide the service: create your account, save and sync your designs, and generate AI responses.
  • To process and manage Pro subscriptions through Stripe.
  • To enforce free-tier limits and protect the service from abuse.
  • To secure accounts, debug problems, and keep the service running.
  • To comply with legal obligations where they apply.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your designs or photos to train our own models.

4. Service providers (subprocessors)

We rely on a small number of reputable providers to run the service. Each receives only the information needed for its function:

  • Anthropic — AI text and image understanding for design generation.
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management.
  • Upstash — the cloud data store for signed-in users’ saved designs and account records.
  • Vercel — application hosting and delivery.
  • Google / GitHub — sign-in (only if you choose to use them).

These providers may process data in the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

5. Data retention

We keep your account and design data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or ask us to delete your data, we remove your saved designs and account records, except where we must retain limited records (for example, payment and tax records held by Stripe) to meet legal obligations. On-device data can be cleared at any time from your browser.
